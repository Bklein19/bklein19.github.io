Sales Transformation

Every company has “Big Ideas” for growth such as new products, new messaging, new channels, improved productivity or a shift in sales culture.

Crimson Rock Consulting Group’s approach to sales transformation accelerates the conversion of those “Big Ideas” into momentum and sustainable results!

Four Phases

Define:

Sales transformation begins with an assessment and planning process.

A transformation plan is created through in-person workshops and virtual collaboration with executive stakeholders resulting in buy-in and commitment to a well-defined sales change program.

Build:

Four categories of sales playbooks are developed in collaboration with "thought leaders" and subject matter experts across the organization:

Strategy – Where to Go

Leadership – How to Coach

Process – What to Do

Messaging – What to Say

Launch:

Playbooks and associated content, tools, and training are delivered.

Momentum begins to build through in-person training workshops, virtual training sessions, and video-based training modules.

Enable:

Sales transformation programs are designed with the end in mind.

Continuous enablement through coaching, training, and certification activities ensure that new strategies, processes, and messaging are fully operationalized and targeted outcomes are achieved.

LV Swiss

LV Swiss, Inc. is a USA privately held high tech contract manufacturing company. They use the latest precision machining, turning, and milling technology to produce unique components to the proprietary specifications of their customers. Their quoting process is designed to meet their client’s tight schedule with their turnaround time in less than 48 hours. All production orders receive an intensive pre-production review. The review includes quality, engineering, material, tooling, scheduling, manufacturing processes and identification of any critical characteristics.

The shop includes CNC Mills and 22 Swiss Type Machines:

  • Haas VMC Mill VF-2SS with 4th axis: 16”x 30”, 24 tool, and 12,000 rpm
  • Haas DM-2 Mill: 16”x 28”, 19 tool, and 12,000 rpm
  • 9 Tornos Deco 10 Swiss Screw Machines with Automatic Bar Loaders
  • 13 STAR CNC Swiss Automatic Lathes with Automatic Bar Loaders
    (SW12R, SA16R, SB16R, SB20R, SR20J, SW20, SR32)
  • Multi-axis machines have X, Y, Z and C axis on both the main and sub spindles
  • Face milling and end working can be done on and off center as well as live cross milling, drilling, tapping and pinch turning
  • Thread whirling, thread rolling, thread milling and polygon turning
  • Capacity to machine bar stock diameters between 0.078” to 1.25” and bar length 3’-12’
  • Part size diameter as small as .018” to 1.25” and length ranging from .03” to 3.5” depending on part diameter
