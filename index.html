Our Focus : Sales Transformation

Every company has “Big Ideas” for growth such as new products, new messaging, new channels, improved productivity or a shift in sales culture.

Crimson Rock Consulting Group’s approach to sales transformation accelerates the conversion of those “Big Ideas” into momentum and sustainable results!

Our Strategy : Four Phases

Define:

Sales transformation begins with an assessment and planning process.

A transformation plan is created through in-person workshops and virtual collaboration with executive stakeholders resulting in buy-in and commitment to a well-defined sales change program.

Build:

Four categories of sales playbooks are developed in collaboration with "thought leaders" and subject matter experts across the organization:

Strategy – Where to Go

Leadership – How to Coach

Process – What to Do

Messaging – What to Say

Launch:

Playbooks and associated content, tools, and training are delivered.

Momentum begins to build through in-person training workshops, virtual training sessions, and video-based training modules.

Enable:

Sales transformation programs are designed with the end in mind.

Continuous enablement through coaching, training, and certification activities ensure that new strategies, processes, and messaging are fully operationalized and targeted outcomes are achieved.
